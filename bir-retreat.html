About the Retreat

Bir. Where the sky
meets the soul.

Nestled in the Kangra Valley of Himachal Pradesh, Bir Billing is the paragliding capital of India — but for us, it's a sanctuary. An escape from the noise, the hustle, the endless scroll.

WanderHer brings together 20 women for 5 transformative days of adventure, deep connection, and true rest. Wake up to mountain vistas, breathe pine-scented air, and rediscover the parts of yourself you may have forgotten.

What You'll Do

Experiences curated for
the wandering soul

Adventure

Paragliding over Bir

Soar above the Kangra Valley on a tandem paragliding flight from Billing launch site at 8,300 ft. A true bucket-list moment.

Wellness

Sunrise Yoga & Meditation

Begin each morning with guided yoga and breathwork facing the Dhauladhar range — the ultimate way to greet the day.

Culture

Tibetan Monastery Trail

Walk through Bir's famous Tibetan colony, exploring prayer-flag-lined monasteries and traditional tea houses.

Trek through cedar and rhododendron forests to panoramic sunset viewpoints. Watch the mountains turn pink and gold.

Community

Bonfire Evenings

Gather under the Himalayan stars every evening. Share stories, sing songs, and make friends for life around the fire.

Food

Himalayan Culinary Journey

From thukpa and momos to fresh Tibetan bread — every meal is an experience. All dietary preferences accommodated.

Questions

Frequently asked

Is this retreat only for experienced travelers?▾
Not at all! This retreat is designed for all women — whether you're a seasoned solo traveler or it's your very first adventure. Our team handles everything so you can just show up and enjoy the experience fully.
How do I get to Bir?▾
The nearest airport is Gaggal (Kangra Airport, IXH), about 65 km from Bir. You can also take a train to Pathankot or Chakki Bank and drive to Bir (~2.5 hrs). Once you arrive in Bir, we handle all local transfers. We'll share a detailed travel guide post-booking.
What is the cancellation policy?▾
Cancellations 45+ days before departure: 100% refund. 30–45 days: 50% refund. Within 30 days: non-refundable, but you may transfer your spot to another eligible traveler. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance.
Is paragliding mandatory?▾
Absolutely not! Paragliding is included but entirely optional. Those who prefer to stay on the ground can explore the Billing launch site area, watch flights, and join the group for the rest of the day's activities — no pressure whatsoever.
What should I pack?▾
We'll send a comprehensive packing list after booking. Essentials include warm layers for chilly evenings, good trekking shoes, sunscreen, and your adventurous spirit. Bir in September–November is mild (10–22°C during the day, 5–10°C at night).
Can I book as part of a group?▾
Yes! We have the Duo package for groups of 2. For larger groups of 3+, reach out to us at hello@wanderher.in and we'll create a custom package for your crew.

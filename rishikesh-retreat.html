About the Retreat

Rishikesh. Where the
Ganga heals the soul.

Nestled in the Himalayan foothills of Uttarakhand, Rishikesh is the yoga capital of the world — a sacred confluence of spirituality, adventure, and raw natural beauty.

WanderHer brings 20 women together for 4 days of morning yoga on the Ganges, white-water rafting, the ethereal Ganga Aarti, forest walks, and the kind of stillness you can only find by a sacred river.

🕉️
Yoga Capital of the World
Practice on the banks of the Ganga with the Himalayas as your backdrop — nothing comes close.
🌊
Adventure & Adrenaline
Grade 3–4 white-water rapids on the Ganges, plus cliff jumping and kayaking options.
🪔
Sacred & Spiritual
Experience the ancient Ganga Aarti fire ceremony at Parmarth Niketan — deeply moving.

Questions

Frequently asked

How do I get to Rishikesh?▾
The nearest railhead is Haridwar Junction (~25 km from Rishikesh). Nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun (~45 km). From either, a taxi or bus to Rishikesh takes 45–60 minutes. We'll share a detailed travel guide after booking.
Is rafting safe? Do I need experience?▾
No experience is needed at all! Our certified river guides will brief you thoroughly beforehand. Safety equipment including life jackets and helmets are provided. Rafting is optional for those who prefer to skip it.
What is the cancellation policy?▾
45+ days before departure: 100% refund. 30–45 days: 50% refund. Within 30 days: non-refundable, but you may transfer your spot to another eligible traveler. We strongly recommend travel insurance.
Is the food vegetarian / sattvic?▾
All meals at the retreat are vegetarian by default (sattvic/yogic food). Non-vegetarian options can be arranged for dinners at restaurants. Vegan, Jain, and gluten-free diets are fully accommodated.
What should I pack for Rishikesh?▾
We'll send a comprehensive packing list after booking. Key items: yoga clothes, swimwear for rafting, light layers for evenings (October can be cool), walking shoes, sunscreen, and an open heart. October in Rishikesh is warm (22–30°C days, 15–18°C nights).

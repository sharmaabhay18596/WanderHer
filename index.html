Our Story

We started WanderHer
because we needed it.

It started with two friends who wanted to travel together — but couldn't find a space that felt made for them. No solo-female-friendly options. No community. Just generic group tours that didn't understand what women actually need from travel.

So we built it. WanderHer is the retreat brand we wished had existed — where adventure, wellness, community, and safety come together in one extraordinary experience.

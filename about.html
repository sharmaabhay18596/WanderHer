Our Story

We are WanderHer

A retreat brand built by women, for women — because we believe every woman deserves to feel free, brave, and deeply alive.

"The world is so much bigger than the corner we've been told to stay in."

— The WanderHer founding belief

How We Started

We built what we
needed to exist.

It started in 2022 with two friends — Priya and Aditi — who had been trying to plan a solo trip together for years. Every time they searched for women's group travel, what they found was underwhelming: generic tours, unsafe conditions, or communities that just didn't feel like them.

So they created WanderHer. A space where the full spectrum of women — urban professionals, new mothers, recent graduates, divorcees, introverts, thrill-seekers — could come together and experience India's most dramatic landscapes in safety, community, and style.

Three years later, over 200 women have traveled with us. We've laughed around Himalayan bonfires, cried at Ganga Aartis, and watched each other become braver. That's what WanderHer is — not a tour company, but a transformation engine.

What We Stand For

Our values are
non-negotiable

01

Safety Without Compromise

Every accommodation is personally vetted. Every guide is background-checked and certified. Every location is scouted for safety. You will never be an afterthought at a WanderHer retreat.

02

Real Community

We don't put strangers in a group and call it community. We carefully curate groups, create structured bonding experiences, and build relationships that last years beyond the retreat.

03

Authentic Experiences

No tourist traps. No Instagram set-ups. We go where locals go, eat what locals eat, and experience destinations the way they deserve to be experienced — with depth and reverence.

04

Sustainable Travel

We partner with local guides, local restaurants, and local businesses. Every rupee we spend stays in the communities we visit. We travel with gratitude, not entitlement.

05

Radical Inclusivity

WanderHer is for all women — all ages, all backgrounds, all fitness levels. We design retreats that challenge you at your edge, whatever that edge looks like.

06

Transformation Over Tourism

We're not in the business of ticking boxes. We're in the business of change. Every retreat is designed to leave you slightly different — braver, calmer, more yourself — than when you arrived.

Quick Answers

How do I register for a retreat?▾
Visit the retreat page (Bir or Rishikesh), fill in the interest form, and we'll confirm your spot within 24 hours. You can then complete your booking with payment.
Can I book for a group?▾
Yes! For groups of 3+, email us at hello@wanderher.in with your group size and preferred retreat dates and we'll create a custom package for you.
What is the refund policy?▾
45+ days before: 100% refund. 30–45 days: 50% refund. Within 30 days: non-refundable but transferable. We strongly recommend travel insurance.
Are the retreats only for women?▾
Yes, 100%. WanderHer retreats are exclusively for women and non-binary individuals. This is core to creating the kind of safe, open community that defines our retreats.
